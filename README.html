Research Interests

I am researching cloud and IoT. I want to help society with cloud technology.
And I want to use technology to solve social issues.
I'm particularly interested in Fog cloud and edge computing in the cloud.
I'm also researching content offload WiFi APs that can access content
other than WiFi APs that access cellular lines and the Internet when there is heavy traffic.



Projects

2019 – 2020 JO SPOT 2 (BOOK SPOT!) Solved the problem of information disparity
due to economic disparity in developing countries. I used the Raspberry Pi to
build an electronic library of out-of-copyright books. Then, we succeeded in
developing an environment where Cambodian children can access the electronic
library on their iPads and PCs and read many books for free.


2018 – 2019 JO SPOT At the time of the 2020 Tokyo Olympics, it was expected
that access to content would become more difficult, assuming an increase
in the amount of Internet traffic in Tokyo due to an increase in tourists not only from Japan
but also from overseas. And as a workaround, we considered WiFI AP, which features content offload,
which is the independent distributed information WiFi infrastructure JO SPOT.
